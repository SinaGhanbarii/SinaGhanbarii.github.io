      title: Solar-Assisted HDH Desalination
      text: |-
        My M.Sc. research investigates a multistage humidification–dehumidification desalination process powered by solar energy. The work combines experimental testing of condenser enhancements with thermal modelling and system-level analysis.

        The project examines how condenser configuration, added surface structures, flow conditions, and heat-transfer performance influence freshwater productivity. A mathematical model is used to connect component-scale observations with the behaviour of a solar-assisted multistage process.
