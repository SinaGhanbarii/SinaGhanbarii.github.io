This B.Sc. thesis modelled gaseous-pollutant conversion in a three-way catalytic converter with a honeycomb structure.

## Objectives

- Represent reaction and transport behaviour within the monolith
- Analyse gaseous-pollutant conversion
- Examine mass-transfer effects
- Determine how operating parameters influence conversion efficiency

## Tools

COMSOL Multiphysics and MATLAB were used for model development and analysis.
