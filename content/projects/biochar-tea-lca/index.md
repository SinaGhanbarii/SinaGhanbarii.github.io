A pilot-scale biochar production process was designed around slow pyrolysis of corncob waste.

## Process design

- Integrated syngas utilisation for internal heating
- Sized key equipment, including the rotary kiln and dryers
- Developed the process basis and material/energy considerations

## Economic assessment

A Class 5 techno-economic assessment was used to estimate CAPEX and OPEX. Project performance was evaluated using NPV, IRR, and ROI, with sensitivity analysis on feed rate and product pricing.

## Environmental assessment

A cradle-to-gate life-cycle assessment quantified greenhouse-gas emissions, including carbon dioxide and methane, and examined sensitivity to alternative electricity mixes.
