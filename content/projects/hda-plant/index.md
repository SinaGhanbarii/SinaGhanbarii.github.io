This project developed a conceptual design for a hydrodealkylation-of-toluene plant.

## Engineering work

- Built the process simulation in Aspen HYSYS
- Used MATLAB for supporting calculations and analysis
- Evaluated reaction, separation, recycle, and utility requirements
- Assessed process feasibility and economic performance

## Economic indicators

The assessment included net present value, internal rate of return, return on investment, and payback period.
